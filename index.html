About us

--Bio--

A gifted new jazz combo on the scene, the Four O'Clock Five has quickly gained the reputation as having a mature, memorable sound and a skillfulness at setting the mood at its host venues. It has played at weddings, company and private parties, hotels, and upscale restaurants in preparation for its upcoming recording at KRTU (San Antonio's jazz radio station) , where it will premiere its originals alongside timeless favorites and cutting-edge jazz tunes.

The Four 'clock Five (n.)a jazz combo that specializes in Jazz, Latin, and Funk music

--Who makes the music--

  • Josh Payne

    Our drummer, producer, and composer has seen success in both the classical and jazz arenas and is now writing originals for various ensembles and in a multiplicity of genres. He has performed as the principal timpanist for the TMEA All-State Symphony Orchestra and has studied under Mike Gomez and Ed Soph. He plans to continue his musical growth at Stanford University next year.

    Mario Dominguez

    Our All-State Jazz tenor sax player is blowing his way through senior year, commanding a mature, inspirational sound in all genres and environment.

    Ryan Whetten

    After only playing jazz for three years, our highly talented guitarist has quickly mastered a sophisticated, mature control of his instrument. After coming first in the region for jazz guitarist, Ryan decided to attend Texas State University to be close enough to jam with the group. Also a gifted computer programmer, Ryan single-handedly designed and maintains the website for the group.

  • AJ Magellan

    Our rock-solid bass player possesses great achievements in both the jazz and classical genres. An All-Stater and music major at the University of Texas in San Antonio, AJ lays down the foundation for the ensemble and sets the mood for any occasion. Just wait till he lets his hair down!

    Brendan Frederick

    Our youngest member, Brendan, is an accomplished alto saxophonist who has played with numerous performers such as the Academy of Fine Arts jazz ensemble, vocalist Dejah Hall, and numerous big bands around San Antonio. He has made lead alto in the area jazz band and is among the top jazz saxophonists in the state of Texas

    Adam Carrillo

    An extremely gifted multi-instrumentalist, our pianist and alto saxophonist has seen great success throughout his musical life. After making Jazz Band of America in high school, he went on to attend The Berklee School of Music and complete a graduate degree at the Jacobs School of Music. He plans on going to New York to become the next Jazz Great.
