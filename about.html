BIO:

A gifted new jazz combo on the scene, the Four O'Clock Five has quickly gained the reputation as having a mature, memorable sound and a skillfulness at setting the mood at its host venues. It has played at weddings, company and private parties, hotels, and upscale restaurants in preparation for its upcoming recording at the KRTU jazz radio station, where it will premiere its originals alongside timeless favorites and cutting-edge jazz tunes.
